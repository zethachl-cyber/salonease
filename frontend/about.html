About SalonEase

SalonEase is an online salon booking system that helps customers book beauty services faster.

Our Purpose

SalonEase helps customers choose salon services, select stylists, and book appointments online.

Our Services

Customers can book haircut, hair coloring, treatment, facial, manicure, spa, and massage services.

Our System

The system connects the frontend, backend API, and MySQL database to support online booking.
